About me

I am a computer science student, fascinated in UX/UI and game design, hopefully future UX designer. I work with Kotlin, Java and Python on a daily basis. I enjoy being part of a team and exchanging programming knowledge with others. Besides IT I am mountain and snowboard lover and cannot lose any opportunity to ride. I also enjoy drawing (mainly cartoon characters) and learning foreign languages, e. g. French & Indonesian.

Education

I started my IT education in 2017, becoming Computer Science student at AGH University in Cracow (Faculty of Computer Science, Electronics and Telecommunications). Since then I learned a lot about programming, I became fascinated with Python, Kotlin and design. Beyond university I took part in a few IT workshops and become a regular participant at PyLight KRK meetups. Nowadays I am expanding my knowledge by taking courses, mainly on Coursera, about UX & game characters design and Kotlin & Web programming.
